Free Engineering Notes Platform

EngiNotes is a student-driven platform where engineering students can upload, share, and download academic notes for various branches including CSE, ECE, EEE, Civil, Mechanical and more. Our goal is to make quality study materials easily accessible for everyone.

All notes are uploaded by students and hosted on trusted platforms like Google Drive. EngiNotes does not host or distribute any files directly.

Why Choose EngiNotes?

EngiNotes provides high-quality handwritten and digital engineering notes for students across India. Our platform covers CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil and other branches. Students can easily search, filter and download semester-wise notes.

Available Branches

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EEE)
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

How It Works

Students upload notes after logging in. Each note is reviewed before becoming publicly available. Downloads are tracked to ensure quality and engagement within the student community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the notes free?

Yes, all notes available on EngiNotes are completely free.

Can I upload my own notes?

Yes. After logging in, you can upload notes for review and approval.

Is registration required?

Registration is required only to upload notes. Browsing is free.
