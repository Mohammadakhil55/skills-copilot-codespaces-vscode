About EngiNotes

EngiNotes is a student-driven educational platform designed to help engineering students access and share academic study materials easily.

The platform supports multiple branches including Computer Science (CSE), Electronics (ECE), Electrical (EEE), Mechanical, Civil, and more. Students can upload and download notes based on subjects and semesters.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality educational resources freely accessible to every engineering student and promote collaborative learning.

What We Offer

  • Free access to engineering notes
  • Branch and semester-based filtering
  • Student contribution system
  • Secure authentication system

How It Works

Users can create an account, upload study materials, and explore notes shared by other students. All uploads are reviewed before publishing to maintain quality and reliability.

Our Vision

We aim to build a trusted academic platform where students can find organized study materials and support each other’s learning journey.
